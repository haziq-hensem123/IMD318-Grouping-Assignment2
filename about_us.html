Synchronization is Key

Founded in Sarawak in 2024, Kaiten Sushi was built on a simple yet complex premise: Sushi tastes best when it arrives at the exact moment of craving.

We realized that traditional conveyor belts were dull. So we created a system that ensures every plate arrives fresh and perfectly timed and a kiosk to manage it all.
